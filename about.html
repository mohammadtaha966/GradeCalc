Our Mission

GradeCalc was created by SME (Studies Made Easier) with a singular purpose: to simplify grade calculations for students worldwide. We provide accurate, instant GPA calculations with complete privacy and zero cost.

Key Features

  • Instant GPA Calculation: Real-time computation as you enter course information
  • Multiple Grading Systems: Support for 4.0, 5.0, 10-point, and percentage scales
  • CGPA Tracking: Monitor cumulative GPA across all semesters
  • Goal Planning: Calculate required GPA to achieve target CGPA
  • Analytics Dashboard: Visual insights into academic performance
  • Save & Export: Save profiles and export professional PDF reports
  • 100% Private: All data remains on your device
  • Works Offline: PWA functionality for offline access

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our highest priority. GradeCalc operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data to external servers. All calculations occur locally on your device.

  • No account required
  • No data collection or tracking
  • No cookies or analytics
  • Complete control over your data

Our Commitment

GradeCalc will always remain:

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions or hidden costs
  • Ad-Free: No advertisements interrupting your experience
  • Private: Complete data privacy with zero server storage
  • Accessible: Available to all students on any device
